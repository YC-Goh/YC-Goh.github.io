#   Marriage Patterns

##  Descriptives

Same-education level marriages are much more common than expected.
This is surprisingly consistent over time.

![Observed vs Expected Marriage Rates by Education Level](/marriages/education.svg)

Similar-age marriages up to groom older by about 5 years are more common than expected.
This is surprisingly consistent over time.

![Observed vs Expected Marriage Rates by Age Groups](/marriages/agegroup.svg)
